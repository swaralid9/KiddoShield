The Power of Child Vaccines : Protecting Our Future

Published on July 1, 2023

On-time vaccination throughout childhood is essential because it helps provide immunity before children are exposed to potentially life-threatening diseases.
In 2021, about 25.69 percent of the Indian population fell into the 0-14 year category. The infant mortality rate is found out to be about 24.5 (infant deaths per 1,000 live births) while deaths under the age of 5 have been about 30.6 (per 1,000 live births).
India has made a whole lot of progress in this field over the years. Despite the progress, infectious diseases continue to contribute to a significant proportion of child mortality and morbidity in India.
